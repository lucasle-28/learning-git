About Lucas

Lucas Le was born in Seattle, Washington, in 2006. Currently, he is pursuing a B.S in Computer Engineering at Syracuse University with an expected graduation date in May 2028. He was a highschooler at Lakeside School in Seattle, Washington from 2020 – 2024 where he was the leader of the Robotics Team, a summer intern with Lakeside’s IT department, and on Lakeside's varsity Crew team.

His research interests are currently still developing but he has interests for the fields of robotics, data security, embedded systems, IOT, and various other related subjects. Mr. Le has received numerous Judge’s awards in various VEX Robotics competitions in 2022 and 2024. He is currently a member of Syracuse’s ASIA club and Cuse’ Baja.
